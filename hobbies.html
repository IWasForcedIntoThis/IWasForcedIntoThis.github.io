Ice Hockey

Growing up in Alaska there is very little to do besides winter sports. So much like my brothers I fell head-over-heels for hockey growing up. I eventually got bitten by the goalie bug and minded the net for most of my time playing hockey as a kid. I grew up watching the highlights of Martin Brodeur, Dominik Hasek, and Patrick Roy. I attempted to emulate them as best I could. In doing so I developed a style of my own like every goalie.

After a serious injury when I was playing as a defenseman, I hung up my skates, but never stopped watching my favorite team. The Montreal Canadiens. Through thick and thin I've been a fan for the better part of 20 years now. It was Carey Price and his injury that actually brought me back to the sport. He inspired me to try it again and now I play adult hockey. No where near the level I was before, but hockey is hockey and for me its blood.
